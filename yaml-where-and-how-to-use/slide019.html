Local Tags

  • Tags with one ! are called local tags
  • In Perl Loaders, they are loaded into classes also
--- - !Dice [3, 6] # YAML.pm, YAML::XS, YAML::Syck # bless ( [ 3, 6 ], 'Dice' )
